THE MARVELLOUS INDIAN CULTURE

Indian culture is the heritage of social norms, ethical values, traditional customs, belief systems, political systems,
artifacts and technologies that originated in or are associated with the ethno-linguistically diverse Republic of India.
The term also applies beyond India to countries and cultures whose histories are strongly connected to India by immigration,
colonisation, or influence, particularly in South Asia and Southeast Asia. India's languages, religions, dance, music, architecture,
food and customs differ from place to place within the country.
